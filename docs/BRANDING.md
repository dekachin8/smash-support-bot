# SMASH Support Bot - Branding Guidelines

**Created:** March 5, 2026  
**Source:** Tim's direction + www.smasheducation.com

---

## Bot Identity

**Name:** Smashy  
**Personality:** Friendly, helpful, encouraging (like the mascot!)  
**Mascot:** Orange character with headphones, blue shirt, waving excitedly  
**Image:** `public/images/smashy-mascot.png`

---

## Visual Design

### Mascot
- **Character:** Orange superhero-like character
- **Features:** Headphones, blue shirt with yellow star, cape, excited/happy expression
- **Pose:** Waving with one arm raised (welcoming gesture)
- **Style:** Cartoon, friendly, approachable
- **Usage:** 
  - Chat widget button (floating icon)
  - Chat header (next to "Smashy" name)
  - Loading states (can animate waving)
  - Success messages

### Color Palette (from smasheducation.com)
- **Primary:** Orange/coral (mascot color)
- **Secondary:** Blue (shirt color)
- **Accent:** Yellow (star on shirt)
- **Background:** Clean white or light gradient
- **Text:** Dark gray for readability

### Typography
- Clean, modern sans-serif
- Friendly but professional
- Good readability for both English and Spanish

---

## Voice & Tone

### Personality Traits
- 😊 Warm and welcoming
- 💪 Encouraging and supportive
- 🎯 Clear and helpful
- 🤝 Patient and understanding
- 📚 Educational (SMASH is about learning)

### Language Style
- **Spanish:** Tú form (friendly but respectful)
- **English:** Conversational, warm
- **Both:** 
  - Positive reinforcement
  - "No worries, we'll fix this!"
  - "¡No te preocupes, te ayudaremos!"
  - End with encouraging phrases

### Example Greetings

**Spanish:**
> ¡Hola! Soy Smashy, tu asistente de SMASH Education. ¿Cómo puedo ayudarte hoy?

**English:**
> Hi there! I'm Smashy, your SMASH Education support assistant. How can I help you today?

## Chat Interface Design

### Header
```
[Smashy Icon] Smashy
              SMASH Education Support
```

### Floating Button
- Circular button with Smashy waving
- Orange/coral background gradient
- Pulse animation on hover
- Position: Bottom right corner

### Message Bubbles
- **Student messages:** Light blue/gray (right side)
- **Smashy messages:** White with orange left border (left side)
- **System messages:** Light yellow background (centered)

### Loading State
- Smashy icon with gentle bounce or wave animation
- "Smashy está pensando..." / "Smashy is thinking..."

## Brand Consistency with SMASH Education

### Alignment
- Use SMASH Education logo in footer
- Link to www.smasheducation.com
- Maintain professional educational tone
- Support the learning mission

### Footer Text
```
Powered by SMASH Education
Learn faster. Go further. Sound native.
```

---

## Dos and Don'ts

### ✅ DO:
- Keep Smashy friendly and approachable
- Use the mascot consistently
- Maintain warm, encouraging tone
- Stay on-brand with SMASH values
- Be professional yet personable

### ❌ DON'T:
- Make Smashy too casual or silly
- Use sarcasm or negative language
- Deviate from established color palette
- Forget bilingual nature (always support both languages)
- Lose the educational mission focus

## Animation Ideas (Phase 2)

### Mascot States
- **Idle:** Gentle sway or breathing
- **Listening:** Lean forward slightly
- **Thinking:** Hand on chin, thoughtful
- **Success:** Arms raised in celebration
- **Waving:** Friendly wave (greeting/goodbye)

### Microinteractions
- Gentle pulse on new messages
- Smooth transitions between states
- Bounce on successful actions
- Friendly shake on errors (not scary)
